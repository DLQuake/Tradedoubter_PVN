About us

Tradedoubler PVN

Tradedoubler PVN is a unique white label solution based on a Private Virtual Network which connects publishers and advertisers in the same central platform. The solution is created by Tradedoubler - an international market-leading affiliate network, with over 20 years of affiliate marketing experience, offering its revolutionary technology solutions. Through Tradedoubler PVN, we provide you with the possibility of managing as many advertisers and publishers as you want directly through one platform.

About affiliate marketing

Affiliate marketing is a type of performance-based marketing in which a retailer rewards one or more publishers (referred to as “affiliates”) for each visitor or customer brought by the publisher's own marketing efforts. The publisher is usually rewarded for a pre-defined action, such as a sale, a newsletter registration, a download or a click. Essentially, publishers act as an outsourced sales team for the advertiser, promoting their products and getting rewarded, most often, on a percentage of the basket value. This means that the advertiser only pays for the direct benefits they receive.
